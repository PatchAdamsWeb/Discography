Motion City Soundtrack

About

Based in Minneapolis, MN and formed in 1997. The band had a couple of line-up revisions over the course of their first 3 years. With the final adjustments made the Pop-Punk Quintet Powerhouse began to work on their first LP, I Am The Movie. Over the years they released 5 more albums, each infused with a different feeling ranging from pop-punk to emo to punk rock. They've released several split LPs with other bands such as Limbeck and The Wonder Years. Amongst other things they've also released a documentary over their first album.

The band toured very heavily over the years to promote their new music or even celebrate an album's release. In 2013, drummer Tony Thaxton decided to step down from the band to pursue other goals. In search of a new drummer, they recruited Claudio Riveria a long-time friend and drummer of Saves The Day. They pressed forward and released their last album, Panic Stations, after this change. As the band entered 2016 the band announced their break up with a statement that read in-part," We have no idea what the future holds, but for now we are done". At this point Tony came back to tour with the band for their final time in the So Long, Farewell Tour. They performed their final show at the Metro in Chicago on September 18th, 2016.
